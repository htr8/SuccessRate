Privacy Policy

Privacy & Security Overview

SuccessRate — Retirement Planning
Effective date: March 2026

Privacy First — By Design

Your financial data belongs to you — and only you. The app is designed so your information stays entirely on your device. There are no external servers, no cloud accounts, and no data collection pipelines.

Your Data Stays on Your Device

All of your financial information is stored locally on your device.

  • No cloud storage
  • No external databases
  • No syncing to third-party services

We cannot access your data — ever.

Local Account — Not a Cloud Account

The app uses a local account for access. Your password is:

  • Stored only on your device
  • Used to encrypt your database
  • Never transmitted or stored externally

Strong Encryption

Your database is encrypted using SQLCipher. The encryption key is derived from your password, meaning your data cannot be accessed without it — not by us, and not by anyone else.

Biometric Protection

You can enable Face ID or Touch ID for convenient, secure access without re-entering your password.

Fully Offline by Design

  • No internet connection required to use the app
  • No background data transfers
  • Updates delivered only through the official App Store or Microsoft Store

No Tracking. No Data Collection.

  • No activity tracking
  • No analytics or telemetry
  • No advertisements
  • No data sharing with third parties

Diagnostics Without Compromising Privacy

Local diagnostic logs are stored on your device and contain no personal or financial information. Any logs shared for support purposes are scrubbed of sensitive data before transmission.

No Automatic Backups

  • No automatic cloud backups

You are responsible for maintaining access to your device and, optionally, creating manual backups of your database file.

Built on Platform Security

  • Device-level encryption (iOS Secure Enclave / Windows Data Protection)
  • App sandboxing — the app cannot access other apps' data
  • Secure authentication via passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Contact us at support@successrateapp.com.

Our Promise: Your data stays with you. Always. No servers. No tracking. No data collection. Just private, secure retirement planning.
